Mega Brain is a Claude Code-powered system built for solo entrepreneurs and small teams who have accumulated expert knowledge across dozens of courses, mentors, and resources — and want to actually use it. You feed it expert materials in any format, and it transforms them into structured playbooks, 5-layer cognitive DNA schemas, and AI mind-clone agents that reason with full source traceability. Everything your system produces links back to the original material it came from.

## How it works

Mega Brain transforms unstructured expert materials into actionable knowledge through a five-phase pipeline. Every extracted insight retains full provenance — you always know which material it came from and where in that material it appeared.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ingest">
    Import materials from any source: YouTube URLs, local PDFs, audio files, or plain text transcriptions. JARVIS automatically extracts metadata (title, author, duration) and stores the material in your `inbox/` directory.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process">
    JARVIS runs a 5-phase pipeline — Chunking, Resolution, Extraction, Synthesis, and Compilation — to break down your content into semantic segments and extract structured knowledge artifacts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Extract DNA">
    Build a complete cognitive profile of an expert across five knowledge layers: their core beliefs, mental models, decision shortcuts, structured frameworks, and step-by-step methodologies.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Agents">
    Generate mind-clone agents that embody a specific expert's reasoning style, or role-based Cargo Agents (CRO, CFO, CMO, COO) that apply specialized lenses to your questions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deliberate">
    Use the Conclave to run multi-perspective deliberation sessions. Multiple agents debate your question from different angles and produce a synthesized recommendation with a stated confidence score.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Community vs Pro

Mega Brain ships in three layers. L1 (Community) is free and open source. L2 (Pro) adds a pre-populated knowledge base, mind clone agents, the full processing pipeline, and the Conclave system. L3 is your personal data layer — your materials, sessions, and `.env` — which always stays local and never leaves your machine.

| Feature | Community (L1) | Pro (L2) |
|---|---|---|
| CLI and setup wizard | ✓ | ✓ |
| Core engine and templates | ✓ | ✓ |
| Skills and hooks | ✓ | ✓ |
| Agent templates and examples | ✓ | ✓ |
| Populated knowledge base | — | ✓ |
| Mind clone agents | — | ✓ |
| Pipeline processing | — | ✓ |
| Conclave / multi-agent system | — | ✓ |

<Note>
  Pro requires a MoneyClub membership. Run `npx mega-brain-ai upgrade` to validate your email and unlock Pro features.
</Note>
