Mega Brain is a Claude Code-powered knowledge management system built for solo entrepreneurs and small teams who want to operationalize the expertise they've accumulated across courses, mentors, and resources. You feed it raw materials — YouTube videos, PDFs, audio transcriptions, podcasts, training courses — and it transforms them into structured playbooks, cognitive DNA schemas, and AI agents that reason with full source provenance. Every insight traces back to the original material so you always know where your knowledge came from.

## How It Works

Mega Brain moves every piece of expert material through a structured 5-phase pipeline managed by the JARVIS orchestrator. Each phase builds on the last, turning raw content into actionable, agent-ready knowledge.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ingest">
    Import materials from any source — YouTube URLs, local PDFs, audio files, or plain text transcriptions. Run `/ingest` in Claude Code to start the intake process and queue your content for processing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process">
    JARVIS runs the ingested material through its core engine, applying 20+ automation hooks to clean, chunk, and structure the raw content for downstream extraction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Extract DNA">
    Run `/extract-dna` to build a full cognitive profile of the source material. Mega Brain surfaces five knowledge layers: Philosophies, Mental Models, Heuristics, Frameworks, and Methodologies — each linked back to its original source.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Agents">
    Run `/create-agent` to generate a mind-clone AI agent grounded in the extracted DNA. The agent reasons, responds, and deliberates in the voice and logic of the original expert.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deliberate">
    Bring multiple agents together with `/conclave` for evidence-based multi-agent deliberation. Use structured debate sessions to stress-test decisions, synthesize perspectives, and produce documented outputs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Playbooks">
    Run `/playbook` to synthesize insights from multiple sources into structured, step-by-step guides tailored to your workflows. Playbooks are the shareable, actionable end-product of everything the pipeline produces.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Features

- **Multi-format ingestion** — Import videos, PDFs, transcriptions, podcasts, and training courses from any source.
- **5-layer DNA extraction** — Structured knowledge extraction across Philosophies, Mental Models, Heuristics, Frameworks, and Methodologies.
- **Mind clone agents** — AI agents that reason like specific experts, grounded in their actual materials.
- **Multi-agent Conclave** — Evidence-based deliberation sessions with structured debate and documented output.
- **JARVIS orchestrator** — Intelligent pipeline management with 20+ automation hooks for quality control and consistency.
- **CLI-first workflow** — Powerful slash commands with session persistence for every major operation.
- **Full provenance** — Every piece of extracted knowledge traces back to its original source material.
